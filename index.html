questions

Things people always ask.

Do I need to create an account? +

No. LinguaHover works completely without sign-up. Free plan stores everything locally in your browser. Pro users can optionally sync across devices with cloud storage.

Which browsers are supported? +

Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Arc are fully supported. Firefox and Safari support is in beta.

Does it work offline? +

Saved words, quizzes, and your local word list work fully offline. Live translation needs an internet connection.

What languages do you support? +

Free includes 50 languages. Pro adds 50+ more including regional dialects like Brazilian vs European Portuguese.

Can I cancel Pro anytime? +

Yes. Pro is billed monthly and you can cancel anytime. You'll keep your Free features and all saved words.

Is my data private? +

Free plan is 100% local — nothing leaves your browser. Pro cloud sync is encrypted. We never sell or read your saved words. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 31, 2026

LinguaHover ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. LinguaHover does not require you to create an account.

1. Data We Collect

Free Plan users: All your data — saved words, quiz results, streaks, and settings — is stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to our servers. We have no access to it.

Pro Plan users: To enable cloud sync, we store your saved words and settings on our encrypted servers. This data is tied to your payment identifier (via LemonSqueezy) and is never linked to personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

We do not collect browsing history. We do not log which pages you visit or which words you hover over.

2. Translation Requests

When you hover a word, a translation request is sent to our translation API. These requests are not logged or stored on our end. The word is processed and the result is returned to your browser immediately.

3. Payment Data

Pro plan payments are processed by LemonSqueezy, a third-party payment provider. We do not store your credit card details. Please review LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

4. Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g. total number of active users, most used languages) to improve the extension. This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

5. Cookies

LinguaHover does not use tracking cookies. The extension uses browser storage APIs solely for your preferences and saved word data.

6. Third-Party Services

LinguaHover uses the following third-party services:

  • Translation API — for live word translation
  • LemonSqueezy — for Pro plan payment processing
  • Google Fonts — for typography on this website

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Free plan data is entirely in your browser and can be deleted anytime by clearing your browser extension data. Pro plan cloud data can be deleted by contacting us at dennisscott6989@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 7 business days.

8. Children's Privacy

LinguaHover is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at dennisscott6989@gmail.com.
