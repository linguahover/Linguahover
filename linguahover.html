questions

Things people always ask.

Which browsers are supported? +

Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc, and Firefox. Safari support is in beta.

Does it work offline? +

Saved words, quizzes, and your local dictionary work fully offline. Live translation needs a connection.

What languages do you support? +

Free includes 50 languages. Pro adds 50 more plus regional dialects (e.g. Brazilian vs European Portuguese).

Is my data private? +

Free is local-only. Pro sync is end-to-end encrypted. We never sell or read your saved words.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Pro is month-to-month. Cancel and you keep your Free features and saved words.
